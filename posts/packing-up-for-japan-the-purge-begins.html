Packing Up for Japan: The Purge Begins!

We got orders to Japan and suddenly everything we own is a problem.

The living room looks like a yard sale exploded. Boxes everywhere, vacuum bags stuffed to capacity, and a "donate" pile that's honestly a little embarrassing — turns out we had a lot of stuff we didn't need.

The rule we landed on: if it hasn't been touched in a year, it goes. No exceptions. It's been brutal but honestly kind of freeing. When you have a hard deadline and a finite amount of space on a shipping container, the decisions get a lot easier.

We've got the HHG pack-out coming up fast, so right now it's full speed ahead on sorting, donating, and figuring out what actually matters. Spoiler: it's a lot less than we thought.
